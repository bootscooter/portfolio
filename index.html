This is me...

I am a graphic-designer-turned-web-designer, providing services in front-end web development as well as graphic design for flyers, posters, social media, and print and (interactive) electronic document design.

Here's what I know...

I am proficient with HTML5 and CSS3, responsive web design, Wordpress, Bootstrap, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, jQuery, and Sass. I’m a savvy user of Adobe Creative Suite, especially Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, & InDesign. I’m also proficient in both UXPin and Balsamiq. and use Git & Github for version control.

I love beautiful docs...

As a marketer for architecture and engineering firms, I have been designing documents —both print and electronic—for almost 20 years. As a freelancer, I have designed flyers, social media posts, posters, and now...web sites, which I find to be both the most challenging and the most fun!

Webbin' it...

Designing for the web is fun and challenging: it takes my love of graphic design, throws in some spice with those added dimensions of interactivity, animation, and connectivity; then wraps it all in the challenge of using acquired and yet-to-be-learned coding skills to make it all work as planned!
