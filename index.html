I am a designer providing services in front-end web development as well as graphic design for flyers, posters, social media, and print and (interactive) electronic document design.

I am proficient with HTML5 and CSS3, responsive web design, Wordpress, Bootstrap, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, jQuery, and Sass. I’m a savvy user of Adobe Creative Suite, especially Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, & InDesign. I’m also proficient in both UXPin and Balsamiq. and have experience using Git for version control.

As a marketer for architecture and engineering firms, I have been designing documents —both print and electronic—for almost 20 years. As a freelancer, I have designed flyers, social media posts, posters, and now...web sites, which I find to be both the most challenging and the most fun!
